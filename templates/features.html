Composition of PlantConnectome


We have surveyed numerous sources to construct our database, all of which are shown on the pie chart to the right.

This database was constructed using over 100,000 different research paper abstracts on plants. Over 350,000 different entities were identified using GPT.

For each research paper abstract, GPT was tasked to identify relationships between the abstract's various entities.

Searching PlantConnectome

PlantConnectome offers five different kinds of searches:


Term Searches

Search for entities in our database by a specific keyword or phrase using this option! Users can also search for aliases, exact matches, sub-strings, and non-alphanumeric results.

Author Search

Search for entities in our database using authors' names!

Pubmed ID Search
Search for entities in our database using the Pubmed IDs of papers!
